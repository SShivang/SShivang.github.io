EducationGuide

EducationGuide is an upcoming app which is set to release in October of this year. This app has many interesting features for its users. Currently, users can fill out surveys and get recommendations based on those surveys. As the system gathers more data about its users, it is concorently generating clusters from which it bases recommendations on. This is in the hopes that students can learn from each other and an attempt to bring social networking into the complicated education field.
